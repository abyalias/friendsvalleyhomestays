Discover Kattapana, Kerala

Kattapana, nestled in the Idukki district of Kerala, is one of the most breathtaking hill towns in the Western Ghats. Surrounded by tea and cardamom plantations, roaring waterfalls, serene viewpoints and wildlife corridors, it is a destination that rewards every kind of traveller — the adventurer, the nature lover, the peace seeker.

The region enjoys a cool, misty climate throughout the year, making it a perfect escape from the heat of the plains. Whether you are trekking through spice trails or simply sitting on a hillside watching the clouds roll in, Kattapana has a way of making you never want to leave.
